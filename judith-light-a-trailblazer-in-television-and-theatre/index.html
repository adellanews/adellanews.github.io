How Did Judith Light Start Her Acting Career?

Judith Light began her journey in acting through the theatre, where she performed in various productions before making her way to television. Following her success in daytime television, she transitioned to primetime with the hit sitcom "Who's the Boss?" where she played the beloved character Angela Bower.
