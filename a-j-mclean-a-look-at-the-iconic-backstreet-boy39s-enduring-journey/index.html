Biography and Personal Details

A.J. McLean, whose full birth name is Alexander James McLean, was born on January 9, 1978, in West Palm Beach, Florida, USA. He is an American singer, and also known for his work as an actor and director. His connection to the Backstreet Boys is, of course, central to his public identity, being a founding member of the renowned pop vocal group. This background, you see, really sets the stage for his remarkable career.

His parents are Denise (née Fernandez, now Solis) and Robert Blue “Bob” McLean. His mother, Denise, has a rather interesting background herself, working as a hospital worker, and also being an author and a motivation coach. This diverse family background, perhaps, contributed to the kind of person he became, someone with many different interests and a strong drive.
