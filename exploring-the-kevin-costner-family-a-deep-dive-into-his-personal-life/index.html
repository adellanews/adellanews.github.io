Who is Kevin Costner? A Brief Biography

Kevin Michael Costner was born on January 18, 1955, in Lynwood, California. He is the youngest of three boys in a family of modest means. Costner's father was an electrician, while his mother was a welfare worker. Growing up, he was heavily influenced by his family's values and experiences, which later reflected in his work. Costner's journey into acting began in college, where he studied marketing and drama at California State University, Fullerton. He initially pursued a career in marketing but eventually decided to follow his passion for acting, leading to his breakthrough in the 1980s.
