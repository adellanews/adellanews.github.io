What is Progeria and How Does it Affect Adalia Rose?

Progeria, or Hutchinson-Gilford Progeria Syndrome (HGPS), is an extremely rare genetic disorder that causes children to age rapidly. The condition is characterized by symptoms such as growth failure, loss of body fat and hair, and a distinct facial appearance. Unfortunately, children with Progeria often face serious health issues, including cardiovascular problems, which can significantly reduce their life expectancy.
