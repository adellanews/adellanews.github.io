What is Kevin Costner’s Biography?

Kevin Costner was born on January 18, 1955, in Lynwood, California, but was raised in the nearby town of Compton and later moved to a suburb of Los Angeles. He grew up in a family of three children, with his father, William Costner, working as a utility executive, and his mother, Sharon Costner, a homemaker. The family environment was supportive, encouraging him to pursue his interests in sports and the arts.
