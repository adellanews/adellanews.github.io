What is Humayun Saeed's Biography?

Humayun Saeed was born on July 27, 1981, in Karachi, Pakistan. He completed his early education in Karachi and pursued a degree in Commerce from the University of Karachi. Before entering the world of acting, Humayun worked in his family's business, but his passion for acting led him to explore opportunities in the entertainment industry.
