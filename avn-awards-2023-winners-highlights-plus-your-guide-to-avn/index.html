AVN Awards 2023: Winners & Highlights - Plus Your Guide to AVN!

Andrew Rivera
• Jan 11, 2026 education and learning

Are the AVN Awards, often dubbed the "Oscars of Porn," truly a reflection of artistic merit and industry innovation? For over four decades, the AVN Awards have served as a prominent platform, celebrating achievements in the adult entertainment industry and influencing trends worldwide.

Held annually in Las Vegas, Nevada, during the Adult Entertainment Expo (AEE), the AVN Awards are presented by the American trade magazine Adult Video News (AVN). The ceremonies recognize excellence across a multitude of categories, from performance and direction to production and technological advancements within the adult film industry. The awards are not only a celebration of accomplishment, but also a barometer of the industry's evolution, spotlighting emerging talent and recognizing the contributions of established stars.

The AVN Awards gala has become synonymous with the glitz and glamour of the adult entertainment world. The red carpet events are often filled with striking fashion statements and the presence of well-known industry figures, drawing media attention and solidifying the awards' place in popular culture. These events serve not only as a celebration, but also as a significant networking opportunity, bringing together performers, producers, directors, and other industry professionals.

In recent years, the AVN Awards have also shown a commitment to inclusivity. The award for Transgender Performer of the Year, for example, highlights the important contributions of transgender performers, demonstrating a move towards greater recognition and representation within the industry. This inclusivity extends beyond performance categories, as the awards also acknowledge advancements in production, technology, and other areas that are driving the adult entertainment industry forward.

Mick Blue, recognized as Male Performer of the Year, and Hollie Hendrix, celebrated as Best New Starlet, represent just some of the individuals who have received accolades at the AVN Awards. These awards, along with those given to directors, cinematographers, and production companies, recognize those who have consistently pushed creative boundaries. The films that win are chosen for their innovative storytelling, production values, and the ability to connect with a large and diverse audience.

The AVN Awards' impact is underscored by its longevity. Since its inception in 1984, the awards have evolved, reflecting the shifts and innovations in the adult entertainment industry. The ceremony now includes over 120 categories, recognizing accomplishments in content creation, production, retail, and web/tech forums. This breadth reflects the ever-expanding scope of the adult entertainment industry, its reach, and the ever growing different business models being adopted.

The AVN Award for Best Actress is a long-standing category that has been a staple since the awards' beginnings. As of January 2025, the current titleholder is Casey Calvert. The recognition of performers like Casey highlights the continuous dedication and talent within the industry.

The influence of AVN extends far beyond the award ceremony itself. AVN Media Network publishes reviews of adult films, tracks industry developments, and offers news releases that keep the public informed about the latest happenings. The publication also sponsors the annual Adult Entertainment Expo (AEE), a major industry convention held in Las Vegas.

The AVN Awards, with their blend of glamour, inclusivity, and recognition of innovation, are an important component of the adult entertainment landscape. They not only acknowledge achievements but also shape public perceptions and inspire ongoing evolution within the industry. The awards also serve as a vital record of the global adult entertainment industry's growth and the achievements within it.
