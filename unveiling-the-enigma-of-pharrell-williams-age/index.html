What is Pharrell Williams's Biography?

Pharrell Williams is a renowned American singer, songwriter, producer, and entrepreneur. His musical journey began in the early 1990s when he formed the production duo The Neptunes with Chad Hugo. The duo gained recognition for their innovative sound, producing hits for various artists. Pharrell's solo career took off with the release of his debut album, "In My Mind," in 2006, showcasing his unique blend of R&B, hip-hop, and pop.
