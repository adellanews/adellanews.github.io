What is Angelina Jolie's Biography?

Angelina Jolie was born on June 4, 1975, in Los Angeles, California. She is the daughter of actors Jon Voight and Marcheline Bertrand. Growing up in a family rich in artistic talent, Angelina showed an early interest in acting and the performing arts. She made her film debut in the 1993 movie "Cyborg 2," but it was her role in the 1999 film "Girl, Interrupted" that catapulted her to stardom, earning her an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress.
