What Are the Signs of Autism?

Understanding autism requires a comprehensive look at its signs and symptoms. Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) encompasses a range of characteristics, which can include:

  • Difficulty with communication, both verbal and nonverbal.
  • Challenges in social interactions and understanding social cues.
  • Repetitive behaviors or intense focus on specific interests.
  • Struggles with changes in routine or environment.
