What is the Biography of Superstar Vijay?

Superstar Vijay, whose full name is Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, was born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, India. He is the son of filmmaker S. A. Chandrasekhar and playback singer Shoba Chandrasekhar. Vijay made his acting debut as a child artist in the film "Kaalamellam Neeye" in 1984. His first lead role came in the film "Naalaiya Theerpu" in 1992, and from there, his career took off.

How Did Superstar Vijay Rise to Fame?

Vijay's rise to fame can be attributed to his consistent performances and charisma on screen. Throughout the late 1990s and early 2000s, he starred in several successful films, including "Kushi," "Thulladha Manamum Thullum," and "Ghilli," which catapulted him to superstardom. His ability to portray diverse characters, from romantic leads to action heroes, showcased his versatility as an actor.

What Are Some Iconic Films of Superstar Vijay?

  • Ghilli (2004) - A major commercial success that solidified his status as a superstar.
  • Thuppakki (2012) - A film that tackled social issues while entertaining the audience.
  • Mersal (2017) - A blockbuster that addressed corruption in the healthcare system.
  • Sarkar (2018) - A political thriller that resonated with the youth.
