Who is Sivakarthikeyan?

Sivakarthikeyan was born on February 17, 1985, in Singampuneri, Tamil Nadu. He started his career in the entertainment industry as a television presenter before transitioning to acting. Known for his versatility, he has starred in numerous successful films and has received several awards for his performances.
