What is Jennifer Grey's Biography?

Born on March 26, 1960, in New York City, Jennifer Grey is an American actress who gained fame through her roles in several major films during the 1980s. She is the daughter of Broadway star Joel Grey and actress and playwright, Frances Grey. Growing up in a creative environment, it was only natural for her to pursue acting. Grey's breakthrough came in 1986 with the film "Ferris Bueller's Day Off," but it was her role in "Dirty Dancing" (1987) that would solidify her status as a household name.

After her initial success, Jennifer Grey faced a series of challenges, including a significant car accident in 1987 that resulted in a rhinoplasty.
