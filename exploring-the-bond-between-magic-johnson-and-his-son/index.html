What is Magic Johnson's Biography?

Magic Johnson, born Earvin "Magic" Johnson Jr. on August 14, 1959, in Lansing, Michigan, is a former professional basketball player and a successful entrepreneur. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest basketball players of all time, known for his exceptional passing skills and versatility on the court. Johnson played for the Los Angeles Lakers in the NBA from 1979 to 1991 and briefly returned for the 1996 season. He was a member of the legendary Lakers teams that dominated the NBA during the 1980s, winning five championships and earning numerous accolades.
