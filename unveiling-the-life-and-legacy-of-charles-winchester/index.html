What is the Biography of Charles Winchester?

Charles Winchester III, portrayed by David Ogden Stiers, was introduced to M*A*S*H in its sixth season, quickly becoming a fan favorite. Born in 1917 in Boston, Massachusetts, Winchester was raised in a wealthy family that valued education and tradition. He attended Harvard Medical School, where he honed his skills as a surgeon. His character was portrayed as snobbish and elitist, yet he had a strong moral compass and was dedicated to his patients.

  • Military Service: Served as a surgeon during the Korean War.

How Did Charles Winchester Influence M*A*S*H?

Charles Winchester's character brought a new dynamic to M*A*S*H, which had already established a strong ensemble cast.
