Who is Patrick Mahomes?

Patrick Mahomes is an American professional football quarterback, known for his time with the Kansas City Chiefs in the National Football League (NFL). Born on September 17, 1995, in Tyler, Texas, Mahomes developed an early love for sports, influenced by his father, who was a Major League Baseball pitcher. He played college football at Texas Tech University, where he showcased his incredible arm strength and playmaking ability, ultimately leading to his selection 10th overall by the Chiefs in the 2017 NFL Draft.
