Who is Wizkid?

Wizkid is a Nigerian singer, songwriter, and performer who has made significant contributions to the African music scene. He gained recognition with his debut album "Superstar" in 2011, which featured hit singles like "Holla at Your Boy." Since then, he has released numerous chart-topping tracks and collaborated with renowned international artists, solidifying his place in the global music industry.
