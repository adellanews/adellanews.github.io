Best Puerto Rican Food: El Cilantrillo Orlando

Eleanor Gray
• Jan 05, 2026 business and economy

Craving authentic Puerto Rican cuisine that transports you straight to the vibrant streets of San Juan? El Cilantrillo Restaurant, a culinary gem nestled in the heart of Orlando, Florida, promises an unforgettable dining experience that celebrates the rich tapestry of island flavors. From their signature mofongo to succulent churrasco, every dish is a testament to their commitment to culinary excellence.

El Cilantrillo isn't just a restaurant; it's an immersive cultural experience. The lively atmosphere, coupled with the friendly and attentive staff, creates a warm and inviting ambiance. Whether you're a seasoned connoisseur of Puerto Rican cuisine or embarking on your first culinary adventure, El Cilantrillo offers a diverse menu that caters to every palate. Their generous portions ensure you leave satisfied, while the rave reviews speak volumes about the quality and authenticity of their dishes.

Name Hiram and Dianne Turull (Owners)
Establishment Year (First Location) 2017 (Kissimmee)
Cuisine Puerto Rican
Locations Kissimmee, Semoran Boulevard (near Orlando International Airport), Florida Mall, Old Town Kissimmee
Signature Dishes Mofongo, Churrasco, Bistec Encebollado, Mofongo en Camarones
Notable Features Catering services, online ordering, delivery, large event space (Old Town location), live music/events (Florida Mall location)
Reference El Cilantrillo Official Website

From the bustling Florida Mall location, renowned for its vibrant atmosphere and live entertainment, to the expansive Old Town Kissimmee establishment capable of hosting up to 450 guests, El Cilantrillo has become a cornerstone of the Central Florida dining scene. Their dedication to quality extends beyond the food itself, encompassing the entire dining experience. Anna, a server frequently mentioned in glowing reviews, exemplifies the attentive and thoughtful service that sets El Cilantrillo apart.

The story of El Cilantrillo is one of entrepreneurial spirit and a passion for sharing the vibrant flavors of Puerto Rico. Hiram and Dianne Turull, the driving force behind this culinary success story, opened their first location in Kissimmee in 2017. Driven by the desire to bring authentic Puerto Rican cuisine to Central Florida, they expanded to a second location near the Orlando International Airport in February 2020, followed by the Florida Mall location and the flagship restaurant in Old Town Kissimmee. This fourth location, a sprawling 7,300 square foot space representing an investment of over $1 million, is a testament to their commitment and vision.

El Cilantrillo has garnered a loyal following, boasting over 3,000 likes on social media and thousands of positive reviews. Diners rave about the "saucy and juicy" Mofongo en Camarones and the "perfect" Bistec Encebollado. The restaurant's popularity is undeniable, ranking among the top restaurants in Orlando according to various platforms.

Beyond the delicious food and welcoming atmosphere, El Cilantrillo offers convenience. Whether you prefer to dine in, order takeout through OpenTable, or have your meal delivered via Uber Eats, they've made it easy to enjoy their authentic Puerto Rican cuisine. Browse their menu online, view popular items, track your order, and explore the latest prices and deals. El Cilantrillo is more than just a restaurant; it's a culinary destination that brings the vibrant heart of Puerto Rico to Central Florida.

For those seeking a taste of the islands, El Cilantrillo offers a rich selection of Puerto Rican dishes crafted to perfection. From classic mofongo to savory churrasco, their menu is a celebration of island flavors. Let El Cilantrillo cater your next gathering and experience the magic of Puerto Rican cuisine. Contact them to discuss your catering needs and make your event truly special. Read reviews, book now, and embark on a culinary journey that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving more.

El Cilantrillo is located at 1301 Florida Mall Avenue in Orlando, Florida. They are open seven days a week from 11:00 am to 10:00 pm. Whether you're a local or just visiting, El Cilantrillo is a must-try for anyone seeking an authentic and unforgettable Puerto Rican dining experience.
