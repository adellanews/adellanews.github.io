What is Vishal Tamil’s Biography?

Vishal Krishna, popularly known as Vishal Tamil, was born on August 29, 1977, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. He is the son of veteran film producer G.K. Reddy and has a brother, Vikram Krishna, who is also involved in the film industry. Vishal pursued his education in the United States, where he graduated with a degree in Business Management before returning to India to pursue his passion for acting.

How Did Vishal Tamil Start His Film Career?

Vishal Tamil's journey in the film industry began with his debut in the 2004 film "Innocent," where he played a supporting role. His performance was well-received, opening doors for him in the competitive world of Tamil cinema. Following this, he starred in several films that showcased his range as an actor, including "Chellamae," "Sandakozhi," and "Thamirabharani." Each of these films contributed to his growing popularity and established him as a leading actor.

What Are Some Notable Films Starring Vishal Tamil?

Throughout his career, Vishal has been a part of numerous successful films that have resonated with audiences. Some of his notable works include:

  • Sandakozhi (2005): A commercial hit that showcased his action and dramatic skills.
  • Thamirabharani (2007): This film earned him critical acclaim and solidified his position in the industry.
  • Pandiya Naadu (2013): A film that combined action and humor, further expanding his fan base.
  • Irumbu Thirai (2018): A thriller that tackled contemporary issues, demonstrating his versatility as an actor.
