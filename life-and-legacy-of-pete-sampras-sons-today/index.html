Who is Pete Sampras? A Brief Biography

Pete Sampras is a retired American professional tennis player, widely regarded as one of the greatest players in the history of the sport. He was born on August 12, 1971, in Potomac, Maryland. Known for his powerful serve and exceptional volleying skills, Sampras turned professional in 1988 and quickly rose to the top of the tennis world. He won a total of 14 Grand Slam singles titles, a record that stood for over a decade until it was surpassed by Roger Federer.
