What is Angelina Jolie's Biography?

Angelina Jolie was born on June 4, 1975, in Los Angeles, California. She comes from a family of actors, with her father, Jon Voight, being an Academy Award-winning actor and her mother, Marcheline Bertrand, being an actress and producer. Angelina's early life was marked by the turmoil of her parents' divorce, leading her to experience a challenging upbringing.
