What is Caitlyn Jenner's Biography?

Caitlyn Jenner, born on October 28, 1949, in Mount Kisco, New York, initially gained fame as an athlete. She achieved Olympic glory in 1976 when she won a gold medal in the decathlon at the Summer Olympics in Montreal. Over the years, Caitlyn transitioned from a celebrated athlete to a reality television star, appearing in the hit series "Keeping Up with the Kardashians." Her journey took a significant turn in 2015 when she publicly came out as transgender, revealing her new identity as Caitlyn Jenner.
