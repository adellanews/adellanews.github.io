The Harrowing Experience of 2015

The core of Alexa Weinbaum's public story revolves around a truly shocking event that happened in December 2015, when she was just 16 years old. She recounts slipping into unconsciousness after being, allegedly, poisoned with a mixture of drugs, including opioids, by her best friend. This horrific act triggered terrifying seizures, leaving her in a very vulnerable and traumatized state. It's a situation that, frankly, is hard for most of us to even begin to imagine. The betrayal from someone she considered a close friend must have been, you know, absolutely devastating.

Lexi claims she was not only drugged but also beaten by a group of people she had, basically, considered her friends. The incident was, in a way, a brush with death, a moment where her life hung in the balance. It’s a stark reminder that danger can, sometimes, come from the most unexpected places. Her survival, thankfully, came with the help of a stranger, a person who stepped in when her own trusted circle had, apparently, turned against her. This detail adds another layer to her story, highlighting the unexpected kindness that can emerge in the darkest of times.
