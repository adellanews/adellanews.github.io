Who is Henry Cavill?

Henry Cavill is a British actor born on May 5, 1983, in Saint Helier, Jersey. He has gained worldwide fame for his roles in various blockbuster films and television series, particularly as Superman in the DC Extended Universe and Geralt of Rivia in Netflix's The Witcher. Cavill's dedication to his craft and his chiseled physique have made him a standout figure in Hollywood.
