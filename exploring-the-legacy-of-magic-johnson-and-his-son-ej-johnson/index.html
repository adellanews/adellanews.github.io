What is the Biography of Magic Johnson?

Magic Johnson, born Earvin Johnson Jr. on August 14, 1959, in Lansing, Michigan, is a former professional basketball player, entrepreneur, and philanthropist. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest basketball players of all time, known for his exceptional skills as a point guard. His career highlights include winning five NBA championships with the Los Angeles Lakers and earning the NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP) award three times.
