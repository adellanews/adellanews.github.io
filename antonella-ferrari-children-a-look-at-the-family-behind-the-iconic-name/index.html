Antonella Ferrari's Family Connections

Now, let's talk about how Antonella Ferrari fits into the larger Ferrari family. She is, in fact, the daughter of Piero Lardi Ferrari. Piero, you see, is a very important person in the Ferrari story himself, being the son of the legendary founder, Enzo Ferrari. This makes Antonella a grandchild of Enzo Ferrari, directly linking her to the brand's origins, which is pretty cool.

Her connection to Piero Ferrari is also important because of the family's ongoing involvement with the company. Piero Ferrari, as a matter of fact, holds a significant position as the Vice Chairman of the Ferrari company. He is also, quite notably, the majority shareholder. So, Antonella's direct lineage to him places her firmly within the family's current influence over the brand, which is a big deal.

It's also worth noting that Antonella Ferrari is a named beneficiary in a trust set up by Piero Ferrari. This trust, the filing showed, includes her along with her two sons. This arrangement, in a way, highlights her place within the family's financial planning and their long-term vision for the family's assets and legacy. It's a clear sign of her importance, really.

The Broader Ferrari Family Picture

The Ferrari family story is, you know, a complex and interesting one, extending far beyond just the cars. Enzo Ferrari, the founder, had two sons. His first son, Alfredino, known as Dino, was born in 1932 to his wife, Laura Garelli. Sadly, Dino passed away in 1956 from muscular dystrophy. This loss, you could say, deeply affected Enzo and, in a way, shaped parts of his life.

Enzo's second son was Piero, born with his mistress, Lina Lardi. Piero is, as a matter of fact, the only one of Enzo's sons who is still living today. This makes his role in continuing the family name and its connection to the company absolutely vital. He is, essentially, the direct link to the founder for the current generation, which is quite significant.
