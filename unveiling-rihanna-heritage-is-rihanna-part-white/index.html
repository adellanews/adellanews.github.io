What Are Rihanna's Ethnic Roots?

Rihanna is of mixed heritage, primarily Afro-Barbadian. Her father, Ronald Fenty, is of Afro-Barbadian descent, while her mother, Monica Braithwaite, is of Afro-Guyanese and Irish descent. This blend of ethnicities contributes to her unique look and musical style.
