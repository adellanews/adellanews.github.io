AJ McLean Biography: Early Life

Alexander James McLean, known to millions as AJ, was born on January 9, 1978. His birthplace was West Palm Beach, Florida, which is a rather sunny spot. His mother, Denise, had a few different roles in life. She worked at a hospital, she wrote books, and she also served as a motivation coach. His father, Bob McLean, was a musician, which, in a way, might have given AJ some of his musical inclination. AJ was their single child, and he was raised primarily by his mother and his maternal grandparents.
