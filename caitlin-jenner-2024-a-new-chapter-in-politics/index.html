What is Caitlin Jenner's Biography?

Caitlin Jenner, born on October 28, 1949, in Mount Kisco, New York, is a former Olympic gold medalist and a television personality. She gained fame as a decathlete, winning gold at the 1976 Summer Olympics in Montreal. Later, Jenner became a household name through the reality TV series "Keeping Up with the Kardashians," which documented the lives of her blended family. In 2015, Caitlin announced her transition, becoming a prominent advocate for transgender rights.
