What is Tenzin Gyatso's Biography?

Tenzin Gyatso, born on July 6, 1935, in Taktser, Tibet, is recognized globally as the 14th Dalai Lama. He was identified as the reincarnation of the 13th Dalai Lama at a young age and was formally enthroned at the age of 15. His life took a significant turn in 1959 when he fled to India following the Chinese invasion of Tibet. Since then, he has lived in exile and has worked tirelessly to promote Tibetan Buddhism and culture.
