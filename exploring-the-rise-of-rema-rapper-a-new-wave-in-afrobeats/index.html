What is the Biography of Rema Rapper?

Rema, whose real name is Divine Ikubor, was born on May 1, 2001. He grew up in a musical family and was influenced by a variety of musical styles, which shaped his unique sound. He initially gained popularity in 2019 with his self-titled EP, which included hits like "Dumebi" and "Iron Man." His music showcases a mix of Afrobeats, trap, and pop, appealing to a diverse audience.

How Did Rema Rapper Achieve Fame?

Rema's ascent in the music industry can be attributed to several key factors. Firstly, his association with Mavin Records, one of Nigeria's top record labels, provided him with a solid foundation and exposure. Secondly, his innovative use of social media platforms, particularly TikTok and Instagram, allowed him to reach a broader audience. As fans began to share his music, his popularity skyrocketed, leading to collaborations with other prominent artists.

Which Hits Defined Rema Rapper's Career?

Rema has released several chart-topping songs that have defined his career. Some of his most popular tracks include:
