What is the Background of Flyn Rider?

Flyn Rider, originally known as Eugene Fitzherbert, is a character from Disney's animated film "Tangled." He is depicted as a dashing thief with a troubled past, seeking redemption and adventure. Flyn's character is inspired by the classic archetype of a charming rogue, and his journey intertwines with that of Rapunzel, the film's protagonist. The film, released in 2010, reimagines the classic fairy tale, presenting a modern twist filled with humor, action, and romance.
