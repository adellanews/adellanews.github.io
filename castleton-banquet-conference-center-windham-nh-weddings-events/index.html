Castleton Banquet & Conference Center: Windham, NH Weddings & Events

Emily Cortez
• Jan 05, 2026 investigations

Searching for the perfect venue to host your next unforgettable event? Look no further than the Castleton Banquet and Conference Center in Windham, New Hampshire, where exceptional service meets breathtaking scenery. For over three decades, Castleton has transformed ordinary gatherings into extraordinary memories, earning a reputation as the premier destination for celebrations of all kinds.

Nestled in the charming town of Windham, Castleton offers more than just a space; it provides an experience. Whether you're planning an intimate wedding, a grand corporate gala, or a lively private party, Castleton's dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure every detail is flawlessly executed. From the initial planning stages to the final farewell, their experienced event planners are there to guide you every step of the way, ensuring your vision comes to life seamlessly.

Imagine exchanging vows against the backdrop of a shimmering lake, surrounded by lush greenery and the gentle sounds of nature. Castletons picturesque waterfront setting creates a truly magical ambiance, enhancing the beauty and romance of any wedding ceremony. With multiple indoor and outdoor ceremony locations to choose from, you can customize your special day to perfectly reflect your unique style and preferences.

Beyond weddings, Castleton is equally adept at hosting corporate events, from productive seminars and conferences to lavish holiday parties. Their state-of-the-art facilities, coupled with their impeccable service, ensure a seamless and successful event, leaving a lasting impression on your guests and colleagues. The versatile event spaces can accommodate groups ranging from an intimate 35 to a grand 800, ensuring a perfect fit for any occasion.

The culinary experience at Castleton is nothing short of exceptional. Their talented chefs craft exquisite menus using the freshest, locally sourced ingredients, tantalizing the taste buds and exceeding expectations. From delectable appetizers to decadent desserts, every dish is prepared with passion and precision, adding a touch of culinary artistry to your event. Don't just take our word for it; online reviews consistently praise the quality and flavor of the food at Castleton, with many guests raving about their "best meal ever."

Castleton understands that convenience is key, and they offer ample parking to accommodate all your guests. With 170 paved spaces in their private lot, and the capacity to accommodate over 200 cars by utilizing the upper lot, parking woes become a thing of the past. This thoughtful detail allows your guests to focus on enjoying the celebration, without the stress of finding parking.

The accolades for Castleton speak volumes about their commitment to excellence. With a near-perfect 4.9 out of 5-star rating on WeddingWire, based on over 100 reviews, it's clear that Castleton consistently delivers exceptional experiences. Guests frequently commend the friendly and attentive staff, the beautiful property, and the seamless execution of events, solidifying Castleton's position as a leader in the event industry.

More than just a venue, Castleton is a place where dreams come true. Their dedication to creating magical experiences is evident in every detail, from the meticulously maintained grounds to the gracious and accommodating staff. Whether you're planning a milestone celebration or a corporate gathering, Castleton provides the perfect setting, ensuring an event that will be cherished for years to come.

So, if you're seeking a venue that combines breathtaking scenery, exceptional service, and exquisite cuisine, look no further than the Castleton Banquet and Conference Center in Windham, New Hampshire. Let their team of experts transform your vision into reality, creating an unforgettable experience for you and your guests. Contact them today to begin planning your perfect event.

And for those special Windham community events like the annual Christmas Tree Lighting and visit from Santa, or the senior citizen events held in the summer, early fall, or at Griffin Park in October (always the Saturday after Columbus Day weekend, with tickets typically available in November), Castleton opens its doors, showcasing its commitment to the local community.
