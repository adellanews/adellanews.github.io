Who is Rema?

Rema, born Divine Ikubor on May 1, 2000, in Benin City, Nigeria, is a talented musician and songwriter known for his unique blend of Afrobeat, trap, and pop music. He gained widespread recognition after the release of his hit single "Dumebi" in 2019, which catapulted him to international fame. Rema is signed to Jonzing World, a subsidiary of Mavin Records, and has since released several successful tracks that showcase his musical versatility.
