Who Are Some Iconic Ugly Muppet Characters?

Several ugly muppet characters have become household names, each bringing their own flavor to the Muppet universe:

  • Animal: The wild drummer of the Muppets, known for his chaotic behavior and distinctive appearance.
  • Beaker: The assistant to Dr. Bunsen Honeydew, recognized for his high-pitched voice and hapless antics.
  • Gonzo: With his long nose and love for the bizarre, Gonzo stands out as one of the quirkiest muppets.
