Ahad & Sajal Divorce: Truth Behind the Split?

Sophia Aguilar
• Jan 10, 2026 environment and climate

Why did the fairytale unravel? The divorce of Ahad Raza Mir and Sajal Aly in 2022 was one of the most talked-about events in the Pakistani entertainment industry, leaving fans heartbroken and the media buzzing with speculation.

The split between Ahad Raza Mir and Sajal Aly, once considered one of the most beloved couples in Pakistan's entertainment scene, continues to be a subject of intense interest and debate. The news, which broke in 2022, sent shockwaves through the industry and left a trail of unanswered questions. The details surrounding the divorce, which came after a marriage that began in 2020, remain shrouded in a mix of official statements, rumors, and cryptic social media posts. While the couple has maintained a degree of silence on the specifics, various sources have offered their perspectives, contributing to the complex narrative of the breakup.

The initial reports were stark: Sajal Aly had legally signed divorce papers and subsequently removed Mir's last name from her Instagram account. This action, coupled with the absence of Ahad Raza Mir at Sajal's sister, Saboor Aly's, wedding festivities, quickly fueled speculation. The digital world erupted with discussions and conjectures, and fans of the couple, affectionately known as "Sahad" (a portmanteau of Sajal and Ahad), expressed their grief and confusion.

Ahad Raza Mir's mother, Samra, responded to fans' messages on Instagram, urging them to pray for the estranged couple. However, the story took another turn when Yasir, a mutual acquaintance, posted on Instagram, alleging that Ahad had not granted Sajal permission to work in films, though she was allowed to continue with her drama projects. According to Yasir, Sajal's refusal to accept this constraint was the primary cause of their separation. Yasir has since deleted the post, adding an extra layer of ambiguity to the situation.

Adding to the mystery were reports of other possible contributing factors, including the alleged interference of Sajal's brother, Ali, and whispers of infidelity. Despite the swirling rumors, the couple appeared together in ad campaigns, creating more complexity and fueling speculation.
