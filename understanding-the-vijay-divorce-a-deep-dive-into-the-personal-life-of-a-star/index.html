Who is Vijay? A Brief Biography

Vijay, also known as Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, is an Indian actor and playback singer who has made a significant impact in the Tamil film industry. Born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, Vijay comes from a family deeply rooted in the arts, which helped shape his career from an early age. His journey in cinema began as a child artist, and he eventually transitioned to lead roles, becoming one of the highest-paid actors in India.
