Best Italian Shows & Movies on Netflix

Andrew Rivera
• Jan 05, 2026 politics

Craving a taste of Italy but can't quite book that flight to Rome? Dive into the rich tapestry of Italian storytelling, available at your fingertips through the magic of Netflix. From gripping dramas to hilarious comedies, the world of Italian television offers a captivating escape and a surprisingly effective language learning tool.

The streaming giant has curated a compelling collection of Italian series and films, catering to a global audience hungry for authentic narratives and fresh perspectives. This curated selection provides not only entertainment but a portal into the heart of Italian culture, its vibrant dialects, and its breathtaking landscapes.

Name Mare Fuori (Sea Out)
Genre Teen Drama, Crime Drama
Setting Naples, Italy (Juvenile detention center)
Original Network Rai 2 (Italy)

One of the most talked-about Italian shows of recent years, Mare Fuori (Sea Out), offers a raw and unflinching look at the lives of young offenders in a Naples juvenile detention center. While the Neapolitan dialect adds a layer of authenticity, it also presents a unique challenge for non-native speakers, many of whom opt for Italian subtitles a common practice even within Italy. This immersive experience plunges viewers into the complex realities of these characters, exploring themes of crime, punishment, and the search for redemption against a backdrop of stunning coastal scenery.

Beyond Mare Fuori, Netflix boasts a diverse catalog of Italian content. From the supernatural mysteries of Curon, set amidst the haunting beauty of the submerged bell tower in Trentino-Alto Adige, to the classic soap opera tropes and dramatic twists of series like Le Tre Rose di Eva, there's a story waiting to capture your imagination. And for those seeking a lighter touch, Italian comedies offer a delightful escape, filled with witty banter and relatable characters. Shows like Zio Gianni deliver laugh-out-loud moments while providing a glimpse into everyday Italian life.

For language learners, Italian television offers an invaluable opportunity to immerse themselves in the spoken language. By listening to realistic conversations and observing everyday interactions, viewers can improve their comprehension of native speakers and pick up nuanced expressions. This dynamic learning experience goes beyond textbooks and flashcards, offering a captivating way to absorb the language organically. Netflix's subtitle options further enhance this learning process, allowing viewers to follow along with the dialogue and reinforce vocabulary acquisition.

Not only can watching Italian television enhance language skills, but it also provides a window into Italian culture. From the bustling streets of Naples to the tranquil landscapes of the Italian Alps, these shows offer a virtual tour of Italy's diverse regions, inspiring wanderlust and deepening viewers' appreciation for the country's rich heritage. Whether you're exploring the complexities of family dynamics in a drama or enjoying the lighthearted humor of a sitcom, Italian television offers a captivating blend of entertainment and cultural immersion.

The success of Italian shows like Gomorrah and literary adaptations such as L'Amica Geniale (My Brilliant Friend) on the global stage underscores the growing appetite for Italian storytelling. These critically acclaimed series have captivated audiences worldwide, demonstrating the power of authentic narratives to transcend cultural boundaries. Netflix's commitment to showcasing Italian content not only provides entertainment for international viewers but also elevates Italian cinema and television to a broader audience, fostering cross-cultural understanding and appreciation.

Even if your Italian language skills are still developing, don't be deterred. Netflix's extensive language options and subtitle features make it easier than ever to access Italian content. Simply search for "Italian" in the search bar and explore the range of TV programs available. You can even add Italian subtitles to many shows, providing a valuable tool for language learners. This accessibility opens the door for a wider audience to experience the richness of Italian storytelling, regardless of their language proficiency. From crime dramas and teen romances to cooking shows and reality competitions, there's a world of Italian content waiting to be discovered on Netflix.

So, whether you're a seasoned Italophile, a language enthusiast, or simply looking for your next binge-worthy obsession, the world of Italian television on Netflix awaits. Embrace the opportunity to immerse yourself in the language, the culture, and the captivating stories that Italy has to offer. From the comfort of your own couch, you can embark on a virtual journey through the heart of Italy, experiencing its vibrant spirit and captivating narratives.

Beyond scripted series, Italian game shows offer a fun and engaging way to practice your Italian. Shows like L'Eredit and Chi vuol essere milionario? (Who Wants to Be a Millionaire?) provide a lighthearted learning experience while exposing viewers to everyday Italian vocabulary and expressions. With their fast-paced format and engaging challenges, these game shows offer a unique and entertaining way to improve your language skills.
