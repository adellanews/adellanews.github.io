What is Nelly Furtado's Biography?

Nelly Furtado was born on December 2, 1978, in Victoria, British Columbia, Canada. She is of Portuguese descent, which has influenced her eclectic musical style. Furtado began her career in music at a young age, showcasing her talents at local events and gaining recognition in the music scene. Her debut album, "Whoa, Nelly!", released in 2000, catapulted her into the spotlight and established her as a force in the industry.
