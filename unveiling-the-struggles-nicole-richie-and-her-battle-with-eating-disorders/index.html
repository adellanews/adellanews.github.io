What is Nicole Richie’s Biography?

Nicole Richie was born on September 21, 1981, in Berkeley, California. She is the adopted daughter of music legend Lionel Richie and his ex-wife, Brenda Harvey. Nicole gained fame as a socialite and reality television star, particularly known for her role in "The Simple Life." Over the years, she has made a name for herself not only in the entertainment industry but also as a fashion designer, author, and entrepreneur.

As a result, her battle with eating disorders became more pronounced during her early years in the limelight.

What Role Did Media Attention Play in Nicole Richie’s Eating Disorder?

The media's portrayal of Nicole Richie, particularly during her time on "The Simple Life," was both a blessing and a curse.
