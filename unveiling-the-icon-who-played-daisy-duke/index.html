Who Played Daisy Duke? A Closer Look at the Actress

The character of Daisy Duke was brought to life by the talented actress Catherine Bach. Born on March 1, 1954, in Warren, Ohio, Bach's portrayal of Daisy Duke made her a household name. With her signature blonde hair, denim shorts, and infectious smile, she became synonymous with the character and helped elevate "The Dukes of Hazzard" to a pop culture phenomenon.
