Who is Jennifer Gates?

Born on April 26, 1996, in Bellevue, Washington, Jennifer Gates is the eldest daughter of Bill and Melinda Gates. Growing up in a family that emphasizes the importance of education and philanthropy, she has always been surrounded by a culture of achievement. Jennifer pursued her education at Stanford University, where she completed her studies in human biology. She is also known for her passion for horseback riding, which she has actively pursued for many years.
