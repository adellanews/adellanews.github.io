What is Elon Musk's Biography?

Elon Musk was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa. He showed an early interest in technology and entrepreneurship, teaching himself computer programming at a young age. After moving to the United States, he attended the University of Pennsylvania, where he earned degrees in both physics and economics. Musk's career began with ventures in software and web development, leading to the creation of Zip2 and X.com, which later became PayPal. His subsequent ventures with Tesla, SpaceX, Neuralink, and The Boring Company have established him as one of the most influential figures in modern technology.
