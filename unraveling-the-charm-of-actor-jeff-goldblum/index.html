What is Actor Jeff Goldblum's Biography?

Jeff Goldblum was born into a Jewish family, where his father, Harold Goldblum, was a doctor's son and his mother, Eleanor, was a homemaker. He has two siblings, a brother named Lee and a sister named Pamela. Goldblum's family played a significant role in nurturing his artistic inclinations, encouraging him to pursue acting from a young age.
