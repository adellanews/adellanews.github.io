Who is Ashton Kutcher?

Ashton Kutcher is an American actor, producer, and entrepreneur. Born on February 7, 1978, in Cedar Rapids, Iowa, he began his career as a model before transitioning into acting. Kutcher gained fame for his role as Michael Kelso on the sitcom "That '70s Show," which aired from 1998 to 2006. He has since starred in numerous films and television shows, including "Two and a Half Men" and "Jobs." In addition to his acting career, Kutcher is also known for his work as a venture capitalist, investing in various tech startups.
