What is Rihanna's Biography?

Rihanna, born Robyn Rihanna Fenty, is a Barbadian singer, songwriter, actress, and businesswoman. She first gained recognition in the early 2000s with her debut album "Music of the Sun" and has since released multiple chart-topping albums. Her music spans various genres, including pop, R&B, reggae, and hip-hop, showcasing her versatility as an artist.
