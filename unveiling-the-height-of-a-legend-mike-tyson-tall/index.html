What is Mike Tyson's Biography?

Mike Tyson, born on June 30, 1966, in Brooklyn, New York, rose from a troubled childhood to become a world-renowned boxing champion. His journey in the sport began at a young age, and he quickly became a force to be reckoned with. Tyson's aggressive fighting style and knockout power earned him the nickname "Iron Mike," and he became the youngest heavyweight champion in history at just 20 years old.
