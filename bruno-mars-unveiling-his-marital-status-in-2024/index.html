What is Bruno Mars' Biography?

Bruno Mars, born Peter Gene Hernandez on October 8, 1985, in Honolulu, Hawaii, is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer. He rose to fame in the early 2010s with his debut album, "Doo-Wops & Hooligans," which included hit singles like "Just the Way You Are" and "Grenade." Known for his versatile musical style, Bruno blends elements of pop, R&B, funk, reggae, and rock.
