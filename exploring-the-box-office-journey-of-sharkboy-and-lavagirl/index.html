What Was the Box Office Performance of Sharkboy and Lavagirl?

Upon its release, "Sharkboy and Lavagirl" grossed approximately $73 million worldwide against a production budget of $50 million. While these numbers seem promising, they reveal a more complex story when compared to other films in the same genre released during that era. The film opened at the box office with an estimated $11 million in its opening weekend, which was considered a moderate success. However, as the weeks progressed, it struggled to maintain momentum, ultimately leading to a lukewarm overall box office performance.
