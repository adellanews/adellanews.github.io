What is Caitlyn Jenner's Biography?

Caitlyn Jenner, born William Bruce Jenner on October 28, 1949, in Mount Kisco, New York, rose to fame as an Olympic gold medalist in decathlon. After transitioning in 2015, she became an influential figure in advocating for transgender rights. With a strong presence in the media, Jenner's life story has inspired many and sparked conversations about gender identity and acceptance.
