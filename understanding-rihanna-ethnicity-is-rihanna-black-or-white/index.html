What is Rihanna's Biography?

Robyn Rihanna Fenty was born on February 20, 1988, in Saint Michael, Barbados. She grew up in a modest household, with her father, Ronald Fenty, being a warehouse supervisor and her mother, Monica Braithwaite, an accountant. Rihanna has two brothers, Rorrey and Rajad, and her upbringing was influenced by the vibrant Caribbean culture. She moved to the United States in her teenage years to pursue a music career, which quickly skyrocketed her to fame.
