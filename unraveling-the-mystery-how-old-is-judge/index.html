How Old is Judge Ruth Bader Ginsburg?

Ruth Bader Ginsburg was born on March 15, 1933, and she tragically passed away on September 18, 2020, at the age of 87. Her remarkable legal career and advocacy for women's rights left an indelible mark on the American judicial system. Ginsburg's age brought a wealth of experience to her role as a Supreme Court Justice, and her decisions continue to influence legal discourse today.

How Old is Judge Clarence Thomas?

Clarence Thomas, born on June 23, 1948, is currently 75 years old. He has served on the U.S. Supreme Court since 1991, making him the longest-serving current member of the court. His age and extensive experience have shaped his judicial philosophy, which often emphasizes originalism and textualism in interpreting the Constitution.

How Old is Judge Sonia Sotomayor?

Sonia Sotomayor was born on June 25, 1954, making her 69 years old today. As the first Hispanic and Latina member of the U.S. Supreme Court, Sotomayor's age reflects a generational shift in the judiciary, bringing diverse perspectives to the bench. Her experiences as a woman of color in the legal profession have significantly influenced her judicial approach.
