Who is Angelina Jolie? A Brief Biography

Angelina Jolie is not just a talented actress; she is also a humanitarian, filmmaker, and one of the most recognized celebrities globally. Born on June 4, 1975, in Los Angeles, California, she is the daughter of actors Jon Voight and Marcheline Bertrand. Jolie gained fame in the late 1990s with her role in "Girl, Interrupted," which earned her an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress.
