What is Sylvester Stallone's Biography?

Sylvester Enzio Stallone was born on July 6, 1946, in Hell's Kitchen, New York City. He is the son of Francesco Stallone, a hairdresser, and Jackie Stallone, an astrologer and dancer. Stallone's birth was complicated, resulting in partial paralysis of his face, which gave him his distinct slurred speech and facial expressions. Despite these challenges, he pursued acting and eventually became one of the most recognizable figures in Hollywood.
