What is Jeff Goldblum's Background?

Jeff Goldblum was born on October 22, 1952, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. He grew up in a Jewish family, the son of a doctor and a homemaker. Goldblum's passion for acting blossomed at an early age, leading him to attend the Neighborhood Playhouse School of the Theatre in New York City. Over the years, he has become synonymous with various memorable roles in films such as "Jurassic Park," "The Fly," and "Independence Day."
