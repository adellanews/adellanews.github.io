Who is Caitlyn Jenner? A Brief Biography

Caitlyn Jenner, born on October 28, 1949, is an American television personality, Olympic gold medalist, and transgender activist. Originally known as Bruce Jenner, she gained fame as a decathlete who won a gold medal at the 1976 Summer Olympics. Caitlyn's transition in 2015 marked a significant moment in her life and the lives of many, as she became one of the most visible transgender figures in the media.
