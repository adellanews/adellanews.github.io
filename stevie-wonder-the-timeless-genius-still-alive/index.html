What is Stevie Wonder's Biography?

Stevie Wonder, born Stevland Hardaway Morris on May 13, 1950, in Saginaw, Michigan, is a multi-talented musician, singer, songwriter, and record producer. He lost his sight shortly after birth due to retinopathy of prematurity, yet he developed an incredible musical gift from an early age. By the age of 11, he signed with Motown Records and began his journey to becoming a global icon.

Stevie Wonder's impact on modern music cannot be overstated. His innovative approach to songwriting and production has paved the way for countless artists across various genres.
