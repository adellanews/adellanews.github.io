What is Actor Goldblum's Biography?

Jeff Goldblum was born on October 22, 1952, in West Homestead, Pennsylvania, to a Jewish family. His father, Harold, was a medical doctor, and his mother, Shirley, was a radio broadcaster. Goldblum's passion for acting ignited at a young age, and he pursued this dream with determination. After graduating from the Carnegie Mellon School of Drama, he moved to New York City to further his career. Goldblum's big break came when he starred in the film "Death Wish" in 1974, which set the stage for his future success.
