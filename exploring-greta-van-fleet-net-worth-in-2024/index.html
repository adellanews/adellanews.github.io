Who Are Greta Van Fleet? A Brief Biography

Greta Van Fleet is an American rock band formed in 2012 in Frankenmuth, Michigan. The band consists of four members: Josh Kiszka (lead vocals), Jake Kiszka (guitar), Sam Kiszka (bass guitar and keyboards), and Danny Wagner (drums). They gained widespread recognition with the release of their debut EP, "Black Smoke Rising," in 2017, followed by their acclaimed debut album, "Anthem of the Peaceful Army," in 2018. Their music is heavily influenced by classic rock legends such as Led Zeppelin and The Who, which has led to comparisons and a devoted fan base.
