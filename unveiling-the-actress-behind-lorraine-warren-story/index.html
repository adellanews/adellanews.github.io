Who Played Lorraine Warren?

In the film adaptations centered around the Warrens' investigations, the role of Lorraine Warren has been notably portrayed by Vera Farmiga. Her compelling performance has become synonymous with the character, earning her critical acclaim and a dedicated fan base.
