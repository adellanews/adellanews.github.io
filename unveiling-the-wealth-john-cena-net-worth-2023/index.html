John Cena was born on April 23, 1977, in West Newbury, Massachusetts. He grew up in a family that supported his athletic ambitions, leading him to pursue a career in bodybuilding and wrestling. Outside of wrestling, Cena has ventured into acting, appearing in films such as "Trainwreck," "Bumblebee," and the "Fast & Furious" franchise, further expanding his horizons.
