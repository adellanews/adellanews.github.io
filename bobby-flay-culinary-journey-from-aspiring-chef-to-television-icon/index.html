What is Bobby Flay's Background?

Bobby Flay was born on December 10, 1964, in New York City. He grew up in a family that appreciated good food, which sparked his interest in cooking from a young age. After graduating from the prestigious French Culinary Institute, he embarked on his culinary journey, working in various restaurants and eventually opening his own.
