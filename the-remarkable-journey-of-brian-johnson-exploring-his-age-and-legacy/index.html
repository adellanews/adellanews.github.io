What is Brian Johnson's Biography?

Brian Johnson was born in Dunston, Gateshead, England. He grew up in a working-class family and developed an interest in music at an early age. Brian's passion for rock music led him to become the lead singer of various local bands before ultimately joining AC/DC in 1980. His distinctive voice and energetic performances quickly made him a vital part of the band's identity. Johnson's first album with AC/DC, "Back in Black," became one of the best-selling albums of all time, solidifying his place in rock history.
