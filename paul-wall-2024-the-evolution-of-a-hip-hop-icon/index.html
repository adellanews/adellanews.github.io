What is Paul Wall's Biography?

Paul Wall, born Paul Michael Slayton on March 11, 1981, is an American rapper, DJ, and entrepreneur. He has made a name for himself in the hip-hop community with his distinctive voice, charismatic personality, and a string of hit songs. Paul Wall first gained national attention with his debut album, "The People's Champ," which was released in 2005 and reached the top of the Billboard charts. Since then, he has collaborated with numerous artists and has established himself as a successful entrepreneur, venturing into the world of grills and jewelry.
