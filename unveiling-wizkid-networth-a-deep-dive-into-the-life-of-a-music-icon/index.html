Who is Wizkid?

Wizkid, born Ayodeji Ibrahim Balogun on July 16, 1990, in Surulere, Lagos State, Nigeria, is a globally recognized Afrobeat artist. He began his music career at a young age and gained significant attention with his debut album "Superstar" released in 2011. Since then, he has collaborated with numerous international artists, including Drake, Beyoncé, and Chris Brown, further solidifying his status in the music industry.

What Are Wizkid's Most Successful Albums?

Wizkid has released several successful albums that have contributed to his acclaim and financial success. Some of his most notable albums include:

  1. Superstar (2011): His debut album that showcased his talent and included hits like "Holla at Your Boy."
  2. Ayo (2014): This album featured popular tracks like "Ojuelegba" and helped him gain international recognition.

  4. Made in Lagos (2020): Widely regarded as his best work, this album features the chart-topping single "Essence."
