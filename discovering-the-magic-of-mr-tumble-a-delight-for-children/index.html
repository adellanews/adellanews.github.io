What is the Biography of Mr Tumble?

Mr Tumble is portrayed by Justin Fletcher, a talented British actor, comedian, and presenter. He was born on June 15, 1975, in Reading, England. Justin's passion for entertainment and working with children led him to create the character Mr Tumble, who made his first appearance in 2003 as part of the CBeebies channel. Since then, Mr Tumble has become an iconic figure in children's television, recognized for his unique style and ability to connect with his young audience.

How Did Mr Tumble Become a Popular Character?

Mr Tumble's rise to fame can be attributed to several key factors that resonate with both children and parents. Why is Makaton an Important Aspect of Mr Tumble?

Makaton is a unique language program that uses signs and symbols to support communication. Mr Tumble incorporates Makaton in his performances, making it easier for children with communication difficulties to understand and join in the fun. By teaching Makaton, Mr Tumble promotes awareness and understanding of different communication styles, empowering children to express themselves in various ways.

What Themes Does Mr Tumble Explore in His Show?

Throughout 'Something Special', Mr Tumble explores a variety of themes that are relevant to young children. Some of the key themes include:

  • Everyday Experiences: Mr Tumble teaches children about daily life activities, from going to the park to baking.
  • Inclusivity: The show encourages acceptance and understanding of differences, making it relatable to all children.
