What is Surya Actor's Biography?

Surya Sivakumar, popularly known as Surya, was born on July 23, 1975, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. He hails from a family deeply rooted in the film industry, as the son of legendary actor Sivakumar. Surya pursued his education at St. Bede's Anglo Indian Higher Secondary School and later graduated with a Bachelor of Commerce degree from the University of Madras. His foray into acting began with the film "Nerukku Ner" in 1997, which marked the beginning of a remarkable journey.
