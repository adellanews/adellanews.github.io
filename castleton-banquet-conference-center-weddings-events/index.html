Castleton Banquet & Conference Center: Weddings & Events

Harper Scott
• Jan 04, 2026 business and economy

Seeking an extraordinary venue that seamlessly blends elegance, versatility, and picturesque waterfront charm? Look no further than the Castleton Banquet & Conference Center. Since opening its doors in 1988, Castleton has consistently delivered unforgettable experiences, setting the standard for events in Southern New Hampshire.

Nestled on the serene shores of Cobbetts Pond in Windham, New Hampshire, Castleton offers a breathtaking backdrop for any occasion. Whether you envision an intimate gathering or a grand celebration, this venue provides a captivating setting that will leave a lasting impression on you and your guests. From the moment you arrive, you'll be captivated by the venue's elegant ambiance, which extends from the beautifully decorated lobby and cocktail lounge to the versatile reception halls and multiple indoor and outdoor ceremony locations. The sprawling gardens, measuring approximately 1850 square feet, offer a picturesque setting for outdoor ceremonies, comfortably accommodating up to 200 guests.

The culinary team at Castleton is dedicated to crafting a diverse and sophisticated menu that will tantalize the taste buds of every guest. From delectable appetizers like crabmeat-stuffed mushrooms and smoked gouda mac & cheese bites to exquisite main courses and decadent desserts, every dish is prepared with the finest ingredients and a touch of artistry. With a menu designed to accommodate a wide range of tastes and dietary needs, Castleton ensures a truly satisfying dining experience for everyone.

Beyond weddings, Castleton is the perfect venue for a multitude of events, including corporate seminars, social gatherings, and holiday celebrations. The dedicated event planning team works closely with clients to ensure every detail is flawlessly executed, creating a seamless and unforgettable experience. Whether you're planning an intimate gathering of 35 or a grand event for up to 800 guests, Castleton offers versatile spaces that can be customized to suit your specific needs. From the intimate Waterview Corridor to the expansive reception halls and the outdoor lawn and portico, the possibilities are endless.

Accessibility is key, and Castleton is conveniently located just off Route 93 in Windham, NH, making it easily accessible for guests traveling from Massachusetts and other nearby areas. The ample parking ensures a hassle-free arrival and departure for all attendees.

Over the years, Castleton has garnered a reputation for excellence, as evidenced by its impressive 4.9 out of 5 rating based on 106 reviews on WeddingWire. Guests consistently praise the venue's elegant ambiance, accommodating staff, and breathtaking scenery. From hosting unforgettable holiday events to providing the perfect setting for corporate gatherings like McLane Middletons annual New Hampshire Employment Law Update, Castleton continues to exceed expectations.

Castleton offers more than just a venue; it offers an experience. The picturesque waterfront setting, combined with the exceptional service and attention to detail, creates a truly magical atmosphere. Whether you're planning a wedding, a corporate event, or a special celebration, Castleton is dedicated to creating an unforgettable experience that you and your guests will cherish for years to come. Contact Castleton today to begin planning your next event. They look forward to creating a magical experience for you.

For detailed directions, Castleton Banquet & Conference Center is located at 58 Enterprise Drive, Windham, NH 03087. Coming from Massachusetts or points south, take Route 93 North to Exit 3. At the end of the ramp, turn left onto Route 111/Indian Rock Road. Continue on this road; Castleton is the last driveway on the right. The main entrance awning extends 33 feet in length and 7 feet in width, providing ample covered access from the lobby doors. The Castleton team is available to answer any questions you may have and help you plan the perfect event. Reach out and let them bring your vision to life.
